<p><ul><li>Solid System</li><li>Liquid System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane rubber can be divided into two main types: solid system and liquid system. </p><p>Solid system polyurethane rubber comes in the form of sheets, blocks, or molded parts. It is typically used for applications where a high level of abrasion resistance and physical strength is required, such as in industrial equipment and machinery.</p><p>On the other hand, liquid system polyurethane rubber is a two-component system that must be mixed before use. It is commonly used for coating applications, such as in automotive, construction, and marine industries. Liquid system polyurethane rubber offers flexibility, impact resistance, and excellent adhesion to various substrates.</p></p>
