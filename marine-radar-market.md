<p><p>Marine radar is typically categorized into two main types: X Band Radar and S Band Radar. X Band Radar operates in the frequency range of 8-12 GHz and is known for its high resolution and accuracy, making it suitable for small vessels and close-range detection. On the other hand, S Band Radar operates in the frequency range of 2-4 GHz and is preferred for longer range detection and better performance in adverse weather conditions. Both radar types play a crucial role in ensuring maritime safety and navigation.</p></p>
