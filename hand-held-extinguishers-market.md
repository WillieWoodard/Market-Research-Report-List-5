<p><p>Hand held extinguishers come in various types such as dry chemical type, foam type, and carbon dioxide type. Dry chemical type extinguishers are effective for class A, B, and C fires. Foam type extinguishers are best for class A and B fires, as they smother the flames and cool the fuel. Carbon dioxide type extinguishers are suitable for class B and C fires, as they displace oxygen and suffocate the fire. Each type of extinguisher is designed for specific fire classes and should be chosen accordingly.</p></p>
