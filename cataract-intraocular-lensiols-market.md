<p><p>Cataract surgery is one of the most common procedures globally. Intraocular lenses (IOLs) are used to replace the cloudy lens in the eye. There are two main types of IOLs - monofocal and multifocal. Monofocal IOLs provide clear vision at one fixed distance, typically distance vision. Multifocal IOLs, on the other hand, provide clear vision at multiple distances, allowing patients to see both near and far without the need for glasses. The market for these IOLs is growing as advancements in technology lead to better outcomes for patients undergoing cataract surgery.</p></p>
