<p><strong>The Piezoelectric Disk Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unimorph</li><li>Bimorph</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Piezoelectric disk market types include unimorph and bimorph configurations. Unimorph disks consist of one active layer and one passive layer, providing low capacitance and high sensitivity. Bimorph disks have two active layers that bend when a voltage is applied, offering higher displacement and force output. Both types are used in various applications such as sensors, actuators, and energy harvesting devices. The choice between unimorph and bimorph disks depends on the specific requirements of the application, such as sensitivity, displacement, and force output.</p></p>
