<p><p>Industrial Spraying Devices are categorized under two main types: High Pressure Cold Spray (HPCS) and Low Pressure Cold Spray (LPCS). HPCS systems operate at high pressures and velocities, creating a strong bond between the coating material and the substrate. LPCS systems, on the other hand, operate at lower pressures and are more suitable for delicate or heat-sensitive materials. Both systems are commonly used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction for coating, painting, or surface finishing applications.</p></p>
