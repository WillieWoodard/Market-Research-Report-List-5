<p><p>Passive tuned absorbers are designed to absorb specific frequencies without requiring external power input. Active tuned absorbers use sensors and actuators to actively adjust and counteract vibrations in real-time. Semi-active tuned absorbers fall in between, utilizing both passive and active elements to optimize performance while minimizing energy consumption. Each type of tuned absorber has its own advantages and applications, making them suitable for various industries and settings where vibration control is essential.</p></p>
