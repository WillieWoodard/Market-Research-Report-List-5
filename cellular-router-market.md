<p><p>Cellular routers provide internet connectivity through cellular networks. 3G cellular routers use the third generation of mobile telecommunications technology to connect devices to the internet, offering decent speeds suitable for basic browsing and communication. On the other hand, 4G-LTE cellular routers utilize fourth-generation Long-Term Evolution technology, providing faster and more reliable internet connections with higher data speeds, making them ideal for applications that require high bandwidth and low latency, such as video streaming and online gaming.</p></p>
