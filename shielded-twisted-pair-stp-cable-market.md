<p><p>Shielded Twisted Pair (STP) cable is a type of copper cable that is surrounded by a foil shield to protect against external electromagnetic interference. It consists of multiple pairs of insulated copper wires twisted together to reduce crosstalk and electromagnetic interference. STP cables are commonly used in networking applications where data security and signal integrity are crucial, such as in industrial environments, hospitals, and government buildings.</p><p>The Shielded Twisted Pair (STP) Cable Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.6% during the forecast period. <p><p>Shielded Twisted Pair (STP) cable market is divided into various types based on different shielding configurations. Apart from these types, there are other variations available in the market to cater to different requirements of shielding and data transmission.</p></p>
