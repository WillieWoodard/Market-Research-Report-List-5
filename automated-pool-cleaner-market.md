<p><strong>The Automated Pool Cleaner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crawler Drive</li><li>Wheel Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated pool cleaners come in two main types - Crawler Drive and Wheel Drive. Crawler Drive cleaners move along the pool surface using a track system, allowing them to navigate uneven surfaces and walls with ease. Wheel Drive cleaners, on the other hand, use wheels to move around the pool, providing a faster and more efficient cleaning process. Both types offer different advantages, with Crawler Drive being more suitable for larger and complex pool shapes, while Wheel Drive is better for smaller pools with simpler layouts.</p></p>
