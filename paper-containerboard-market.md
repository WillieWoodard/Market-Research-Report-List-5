<p><p>Paper containerboard is commonly divided into two main types: Kraftliner and Testliner. Kraftliner is a high-quality paperboard used for the top and bottom layers of cardboard boxes, providing strength and durability. On the other hand, Testliner is a lower-quality paperboard typically used for the inner layers of boxes, offering a cost-effective option for packaging materials. Both types play crucial roles in the packaging industry, catering to different needs and preferences among manufacturers and consumers.</p></p>
