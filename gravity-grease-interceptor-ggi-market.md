<p><p>A Gravity Grease Interceptor (GGI) is a plumbing device used in commercial kitchens to prevent fats, oils, and grease (FOG) from entering the sewer system. The GGI works by allowing greasy wastewater to cool, causing the FOG to solidify and separate from the water. This solid waste can then be easily removed, preventing blockages and unnecessary strain on the sewage system.</p><p>The Gravity Grease Interceptor (GGI) Market is experiencing rapid growth due to increasing awareness about the environmental impact of FOG in wastewater.
