<p><p>The Facial Pump Bottle market consists of two main types: Airless Facial Pumps and Atmospheric Facial Pumps. Airless Facial Pumps use a vacuum system to dispense the product without letting air in, which helps to maintain the integrity and longevity of the product. On the other hand, Atmospheric Facial Pumps rely on the external air pressure to dispense the product. Both types of pumps cater to different consumer preferences and product requirements in the cosmetics and skincare industry.</p></p>
