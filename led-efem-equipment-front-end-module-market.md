<p><p>LED EFEM (Equipment Front End Module) market types include 2 FOUP Wide, 3 FOUP Wide, and 4 FOUP Wide configurations. The 2 FOUP Wide EFEM can handle two FOUPs simultaneously, the 3 FOUP Wide EFEM can handle three FOUPs, and the 4 FOUP Wide EFEM can handle four FOUPs at the same time, catering to varying throughput and automation requirements in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
