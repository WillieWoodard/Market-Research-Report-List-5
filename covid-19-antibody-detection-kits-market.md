RDT kits provide quick results by detecting antibodies in blood samples. ELISA kits offer a more accurate and sensitive detection method using enzyme-linked antibodies. Neutralization Assay kits measure the ability of antibodies to neutralize the virus. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences, providing healthcare professionals with diverse options for COVID-19 antibody testing.</p></p>
