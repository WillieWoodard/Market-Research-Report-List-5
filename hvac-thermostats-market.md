<p><p>HVAC Thermostats come in three main types: Plug-In Type Thermostats, Immersion Type Thermostats, and Surface Type Thermostats. Plug-In Type Thermostats are easy to install and can be plugged into an electrical outlet. Immersion Type Thermostats are designed to be immersed in the fluid they are regulating the temperature of. Surface Type Thermostats are mounted on the surface of the object they are controlling the temperature of. Each type caters to different HVAC systems and preferences, offering a range of options for temperature control.</p></p>
