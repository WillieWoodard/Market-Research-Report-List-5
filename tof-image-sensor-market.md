<p><p>ToF Image Sensors can be classified into two main types: Direct ToF Sensors and Indirect ToF Sensors. </p><p>Direct ToF Sensors directly measure the time it takes for light to travel from the sensor to the object and back, providing accurate depth information. On the other hand, Indirect ToF Sensors use techniques like triangulation or pattern projection to calculate depth information, which may not be as accurate but can be more cost-effective. Both types of sensors have their own advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
