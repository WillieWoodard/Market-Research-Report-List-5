<p><p>EAA adhesive market can be categorized into Film Grade, Extrusion Coating Grade, and Dispersible Grade. Film Grade adhesives are used in packaging films for bonding different layers together. Extrusion Coating Grade adhesives are applied on substrates like paper or plastics for improving barrier properties. Dispersible Grade adhesives are designed to disperse easily in water, making them ideal for applications like paper coating and construction industry. Each grade caters to specific needs and requirements in different industries.</p></p>
