<p><p>Online payroll services are offered in two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise payroll solutions require businesses to maintain software on their own servers, providing greater control over data but also requiring more in-house resources. Cloud-based payroll services, on the other hand, store data on remote servers accessed via the internet, offering flexibility and scalability without the need for extensive IT infrastructure. Both options cater to different business needs and preferences, with cloud-based services becoming increasingly popular for their convenience and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
