<p><p>Commercial infant formulas are categorized into four main types based on their protein source. Cow milk protein-based formulas are the most commonly used and closely resemble breast milk in composition. Soy-based formulas are suitable for infants with lactose intolerance or cow milk allergies. Protein hydrolysate formulas are hypoallergenic and easier to digest. Other types include special formulas for preterm infants or those with specific medical conditions. Each type serves a unique purpose in providing nutrition for infants who cannot be breastfed.</p></p>
