<p><strong>The Railway Shock Absorbers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mono-tube</li><li>Twin-tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Railway shock absorbers can be classified into two main types - Mono-tube and Twin-tube. Mono-tube shock absorbers have a single tube design, with the piston and oil chamber contained within the same tube. They are generally preferred for their better heat dissipation and responsiveness. On the other hand, Twin-tube shock absorbers have an outer tube housing the oil chamber and an inner tube containing the piston. These are known for their reliability and durability over rough terrains. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements in the railway shock absorber market.</p></p>
