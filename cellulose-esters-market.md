<p><p>Cellulose esters are a type of derivative of cellulose, a natural polymer found in plants. The three main types of cellulose esters are Cellulose Acetate, Cellulose Acetate Butyrate, Cellulose Acetate Propionate, and Cellulose Nitrate. These esters are widely used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, food packaging, textiles, and coatings. Cellulose Acetate is known for its high clarity and good solvent resistance, Cellulose Acetate Butyrate offers improved flexibility, Cellulose Acetate Propionate provides enhanced durability, and Cellulose Nitrate is known for its fast-drying properties.</p></p>
