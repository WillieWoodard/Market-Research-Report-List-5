<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single component glass cement is divided into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I is a high-performance glass cement designed for applications such as structural glazing, curtain walls, and other demanding architectural projects. It has strong adhesion and weather resistance properties, making it ideal for long-lasting installations. On the other hand, Type II is a more general-purpose glass cement suitable for a wide range of non-structural applications like bonding glass to non-porous materials. Both types offer reliable bonding strength and durability for different project requirements.</p></p>
