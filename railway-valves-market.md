<p><p>Railway valves market is categorized into various types such as Gate Valves, Control Valves, Ball Valves, Butterfly Valves, and Others. Gate valves are used to control flow, while Control valves regulate the flow of fluids. Ball valves provide tight shut-off and are suitable for high-pressure applications.
