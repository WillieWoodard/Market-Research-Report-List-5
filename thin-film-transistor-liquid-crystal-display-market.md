<p><p>Thin Film Transistor Liquid Crystal Display (TFT LCD) technology is classified into three main types: Twisted Nematic (TN), Vertical Alignment (VA), and In-Plane Switching (IPS). TN displays offer fast response times and low cost, suitable for basic applications. VA displays provide better color reproduction and wider viewing angles, making them popular for TVs and monitors. IPS displays offer the highest image quality and viewing angles, making them ideal for high-end devices such as smartphones and professional monitors. Each market type caters to specific needs and preferences of consumers.</p></p>
