<p><p>Riveted gratings come in two main market types: smooth surface and blade surface. Smooth surface gratings are designed with a flat top surface, making them ideal for applications where high slip resistance is not required. On the other hand, blade surface gratings feature serrated tops for enhanced traction, making them suitable for environments where slip resistance is crucial. Both types of riveted gratings offer durability and strength, making them popular choices for various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
