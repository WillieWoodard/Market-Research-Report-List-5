<p><p>Solvent-based laminating adhesives are commonly used in industrial applications due to their fast drying time and strong bond. They contain volatile organic compounds which evaporate during the drying process. On the other hand, solventless laminating adhesives are gaining popularity in the industrial market as they are more eco-friendly and do not contain harmful VOCs. These adhesives rely on chemical reactions for curing, resulting in a strong and durable bond without the need for additional drying time.</p></p>
