<p><ul><li>FSI Technology</li><li>BSI Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMOS Image Transducers are used in various applications like digital cameras and smartphone cameras. There are two main types of CMOS Image Transducers in the market: FSI Technology and BSI Technology. FSI (Front-side illuminated) technology involves placing the light-sensitive elements in front of the electronics. BSI (Back-side illuminated) technology, on the other hand, involves flipping the sensor so that the light-sensitive elements are closer to the light source. Both technologies have their advantages and are used in different products based on their requirements.</p></p>
