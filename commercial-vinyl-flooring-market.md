<p><p>Commercial vinyl flooring market includes two main types: Heterogeneous Sheet Flooring and Homogeneous Sheet Flooring. Heterogeneous Sheet Flooring is made up of multiple layers, allowing for a wide range of designs and patterns. It is durable and resistant to wear and tear, making it suitable for high-traffic areas. On the other hand, Homogeneous Sheet Flooring is made of a single layer throughout, providing uniform color and pattern. It is also durable and easy to maintain, making it a popular choice for commercial spaces.</p></p>
