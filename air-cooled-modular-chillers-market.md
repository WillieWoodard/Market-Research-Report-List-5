<p><p>Air-cooled modular chillers are categorized into two main market types: single-cooling type and heat pump type. Single-cooling type chillers are designed to provide cooling only, making them ideal for applications where heat rejection is the primary requirement. On the other hand, heat pump type chillers are capable of providing both cooling and heating functions, offering a more versatile solution for buildings or facilities that require both cooling and heating throughout the year. These different market types cater to a wide range of cooling and heating needs in various applications.</p></p>
