<p><p>The dry running rotary vane vacuum pumps market is segmented into two types: single stage and two stage. Single stage pumps have one rotor that rotates within the pump casing, creating suction and compression in one stage. Two stage pumps have two rotors that work in tandem, with the first rotor creating initial suction and compression, and the second rotor further compressing the air. Both types are essential for various industrial applications requiring reliable and efficient vacuum pumping systems.</p></p>
