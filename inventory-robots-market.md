<p><p>Inventory robots can be classified into two main market types: Mobile Robotics and Fixed Robotics. Mobile Robotics are designed to navigate freely within a given space to carry out inventory tasks efficiently. On the other hand, Fixed Robotics are stationary systems that are specifically set up for inventory management in a fixed location. While Mobile Robotics offer flexibility and agility, Fixed Robotics provide stability and precision for inventory management processes. These two market types cater to different needs and requirements in the inventory management industry.</p></p>
