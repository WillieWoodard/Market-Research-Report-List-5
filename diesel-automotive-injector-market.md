<p><strong>The Diesel Automotive Injector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Throttle Body Injection</li><li>Multipoint Injection</li><li>Direct Injection</li><li>Common Rail Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Throttle Body Injection is a type of fuel injection system where fuel is injected directly into the throttle body. Multipoint Injection involves injecting fuel into each cylinder separately, resulting in more precise fuel delivery. Direct Injection injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber for improved fuel efficiency and power. Common Rail Injection system uses a high-pressure pump to deliver fuel to a common rail that then distributes it to individual injectors. Each type of injector system offers unique benefits and performance characteristics for diesel vehicles.</p></p>
