<p><p>Industrial spray humidifiers can be divided into two main market types: stationary and portable. Stationary humidifiers are typically larger units that are installed in a specific location such as a factory or manufacturing facility. They provide a consistent level of humidity in a fixed area. Portable humidifiers are smaller units that can be easily moved from one location to another, making them ideal for smaller spaces or temporary humidity needs. Both types of humidifiers are essential in maintaining optimal humidity levels in industrial settings.</p></p>
