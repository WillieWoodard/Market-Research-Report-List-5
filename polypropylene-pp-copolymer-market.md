<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Polypropylene (PP) Copolymer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotactic Polypropylene</li><li>Atactic Polypropylene</li><li>Syndiotactic Polypropylene</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polypropylene (PP) copolymer market is categorized into three types based on the arrangement of propylene monomers in the polymer chain. Isotactic Polypropylene has all propylene units in a regular, repeating pattern, giving it a high degree of crystallinity and stiffness. Atactic Polypropylene has randomly arranged propylene units, resulting in an amorphous structure with low crystallinity and flexibility. Syndiotactic Polypropylene has alternate arrangement of propylene units, offering a balance between stiffness and impact resistance.</p></p>
