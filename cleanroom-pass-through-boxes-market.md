<p><p>Cleanroom pass through boxes are essential components in cleanroom environments to ensure proper transfer of materials without contaminating the controlled space. The market for cleanroom pass through boxes can be categorized into two types: static and dynamic. </p><p>Static pass through boxes are designed to maintain the cleanliness of the cleanroom by allowing one-way transfer of materials. Dynamic pass through boxes, on the other hand, feature additional features such as air showers or UV lights to further decontaminate materials before they enter the cleanroom. Both types cater to specific needs and requirements of cleanroom facilities.</p></p>
