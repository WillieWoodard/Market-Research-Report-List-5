<p><p>Meat smokers are equipment used for cooking meat through the process of smoking. There are three main types of meat smokers in the market: gas smokers, electric smokers, and charcoal smokers. Gas smokers use propane or natural gas as fuel, providing consistent and controllable heat. Electric smokers are easy to use and maintain, as they simply plug into an electrical outlet. Charcoal smokers use charcoal briquettes or lump charcoal to generate smoke and heat, giving the meat a traditional smoky flavor. Each type of smoker has its own advantages and is preferred by different individuals based on their cooking preferences.</p></p>
