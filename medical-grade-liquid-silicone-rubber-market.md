<p><p>Medical-grade liquid silicone rubber is categorized into three market types based on the duration of contact with the human body: Limited Exposure, Prolonged Exposure, and Permanent Contact. Limited Exposure refers to brief contact with the body, such as with diagnostic tools or short-term medical devices. Prolonged Exposure involves constant contact for an extended period, such as with catheters or implants. Permanent Contact indicates continuous interaction with the body, like with pacemakers or prosthetics. Each market type requires specific formulations and properties to meet the unique performance requirements.</p></p>
