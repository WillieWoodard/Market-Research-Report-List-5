<p><p>Stationary Automated Waste Collection System includes two main market types: Underground Pipelines and Above-ground Pipelines. Underground Pipelines involves installing a network of pipes beneath the ground to collect and transport waste to a central collection point. This type of system is convenient and efficient, with waste being out of sight and reducing unpleasant odors. Above-ground Pipelines consist of pipes installed above the ground for waste collection, offering similar benefits as the underground system but with potentially lower installation costs and easier access for maintenance.</p></p>
