<p><p>Intelligent gas meters are divided into different market types based on their technology and mechanism. Diaphragm, turbine, rotary piston, thermal mass flow, and ultrasonic meters are all common types in the market. Diaphragm meters use a flexible membrane to measure gas flow. Turbine meters have a spinning rotor to measure gas flow. Rotary piston meters use rotating pistons. Thermal mass flow meters measure gas flow by heating it. Ultrasonic meters use sound waves to measure gas flow accurately. Each type offers unique benefits and is suitable for different applications.</p></p>
