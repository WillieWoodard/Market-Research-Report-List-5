<p><p>Pneumatic Conveying Equipment market is segmented into Positive Pressure Conveying, Vacuum Pressure Conveying, and Combined Conveying. Positive Pressure Conveying uses air pressure to push materials through a pipeline, while Vacuum Pressure Conveying uses suction to pull materials. Combined Conveying systems utilize both positive and vacuum pressures to transport materials efficiently. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications based on factors such as material type, distance, and required throughput.</p></p>
