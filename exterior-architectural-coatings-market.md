<p><strong>The Exterior Architectural Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-Borne</li><li>Water-Borne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Exterior Architectural Coatings market can be classified into two main types: Solvent-Borne and Water-Borne coatings. Solvent-Borne coatings are formulated with organic solvents that evaporate during the drying process, leaving behind a protective film. On the other hand, Water-Borne coatings use water as a carrier instead of solvents, making them more environmentally friendly and safer to use. Both types of coatings offer protection and aesthetic appeal to exterior surfaces, but Water-Borne coatings are gaining popularity due to their low VOC content and ease of application.</p></p>
