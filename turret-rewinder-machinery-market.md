<p><p>Turret rewinder machinery is available in two main types: Semi-Automatic and Fully-Automatic. Semi-Automatic turret rewinders require some manual intervention during the rewinding process, such as changing the core or cutting the finished roll. Fully-Automatic turret rewinders, on the other hand, are equipped with advanced technology that allows for automatic core loading, cutting, and packaging of the finished rolls without the need for manual intervention. These two types cater to different production needs and automation levels in the market.</p></p>
