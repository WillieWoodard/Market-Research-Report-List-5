<p><p>Medium Truck Mounted Crane market includes three main types: Folding Boom, Telescopic Boom, and Truss Boom. Folding Boom cranes are compact and able to reach tight spaces due to their folding mechanism. Telescopic Boom cranes have a series of nested tubes that extend and retract to reach varying heights. Truss Boom cranes use lattice-like structures to provide strength and stability when lifting heavy loads. Each type offers different advantages depending on the specific needs of the user.</p></p>
