<p><strong>The Automotive HVAC Heat Exchanger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Condenser</li><li>Evaporator</li><li>Heat Core</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive HVAC heat exchangers include three main types: condensers, evaporators, and heat cores. Condensers are responsible for removing heat from the refrigerant gas, converting it into a high-pressure liquid. Evaporators are designed to absorb heat from the surrounding air, causing the refrigerant liquid to evaporate and cool down the air inside the vehicle. Heat cores are responsible for transferring heat between the refrigerant and the air, helping to regulate the temperature inside the vehicle. These components work together to ensure efficient heating and cooling in automotive HVAC systems.</p></p>
