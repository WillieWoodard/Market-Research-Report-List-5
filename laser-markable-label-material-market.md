<p><p>Laser markable label material is available in two main types: antistatic and non-antistatic. Antistatic materials are designed to dissipate static electricity, making them ideal for use in environments where static discharge could damage sensitive electronic components. On the other hand, non-antistatic materials do not have this special property and are used in applications where static electricity is not a concern. Both types of materials offer high-quality laser marking capabilities for clear, durable labeling solutions.</p></p>
