<p><p>Functional fragrances market can be divided into two main types: natural fragrances and artificial fragrances. Natural fragrances are derived from plant-based ingredients, such as essential oils, and are perceived as being more environmentally friendly and healthier for consumers. On the other hand, artificial fragrances are chemically synthesized in laboratories to mimic the scents of natural ingredients. They are often more cost-effective to produce and allow for a wider range of scent options. Both types of fragrances play a key role in the functional fragrances market, catering to different consumer preferences and needs.</p></p>
