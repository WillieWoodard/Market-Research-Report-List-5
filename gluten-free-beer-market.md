<p><p>Gluten-free beer is a type of beer that is brewed without the use of barley, wheat, or rye - ingredients that contain gluten. These beers appeal to individuals with gluten intolerances or celiac disease who are unable to consume traditional beer.</p><p>The Gluten-free Beer Market is experiencing significant growth, with an expected CAGR of 6.5% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by increasing awareness and diagnosis of gluten intolerances, as well as a growing demand for gluten-free options among consumers who are looking for healthier alternatives. Additionally, the rise of craft breweries and innovative brewing techniques have also contributed to the expansion of the gluten-free beer market.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the Gluten-free Beer Market include the introduction of new flavors and styles of gluten-free beers to cater to different preferences, as well as the incorporation of local and organic ingredients. Moreover, collaborations between breweries and food companies to create gluten-free beer pairings have also been gaining popularity among consumers. With the increasing demand for gluten-free products, the Gluten-free Beer Market is expected to continue its growth trajectory in the coming years.</p></p>
