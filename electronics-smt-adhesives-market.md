<p><p>Electronics SMT adhesives are used in the assembly of surface mount devices in electronic circuits. There are five main types of SMT adhesives: acrylic-based, cyanoacrylate-based, epoxy-based, silicone-based, and polyurethane-based. Acrylic-based adhesives are known for their versatility and quick curing time. Cyanoacrylate-based adhesives offer high strength bonding. Epoxy-based adhesives provide excellent chemical and temperature resistance. Silicone-based adhesives are flexible and have good thermal conductivity. Polyurethane-based adhesives are ideal for applications requiring high flexibility and durability.</p></p>
