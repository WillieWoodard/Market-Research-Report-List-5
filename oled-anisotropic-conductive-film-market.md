Chip on Glass involves mounting the chip directly on the glass substrate, while Chip on Flex connects the chip to a flexible circuit. Chip on Board integrates the chip into a circuit board, Flex on Glass involves a flexible circuit attached to glass, Flex on Flex refers to a flexible circuit on another flexible material, and Flex on Board connects a flexible circuit to a circuit board.</p></p>
