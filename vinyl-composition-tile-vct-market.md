<p><p>Vinyl Composition Tile (VCT) is available in two main market types: Single Layer and Multilayer. Single Layer VCT consists of one layer of vinyl, making it a more budget-friendly option but with limited design options. Multilayer VCT, on the other hand, features a backing layer, a core layer for stability, and a top layer for design and durability. This type of VCT offers more customization options and enhanced durability, making it suitable for high-traffic areas.</p></p>
