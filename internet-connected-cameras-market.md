<p><p>The internet-connected cameras market consists of two main types: wired Wifi cameras and wireless Wifi cameras. Wired Wifi cameras require a physical connection to a power source and the internet via an Ethernet cable, offering a more stable connection but less flexibility in placement. On the other hand, wireless Wifi cameras connect to the internet through Wifi, providing more freedom in positioning but potentially less reliable connectivity. Both types offer users the ability to remotely monitor their surroundings through a smartphone or computer.</p></p>
