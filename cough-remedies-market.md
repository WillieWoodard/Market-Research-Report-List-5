<p><p>Cough remedies market can be categorized into different types based on their mechanisms of action. Antihistamines help reduce allergy-related coughing by blocking histamine receptors, while expectorants thin mucus to make it easier to cough up. Bronchodilators help open up airways to relieve coughing caused by asthma or COPD. Decongestants reduce nasal congestion, which can help alleviate coughing. Antibiotics are used to treat coughs caused by bacterial infections. Each type of remedy targets different underlying causes of coughing.</p></p>
