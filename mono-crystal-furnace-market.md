<p><strong>The Mono-Crystal Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Czochralski (CZ) Method Furnace</li><li>Floating Zone (FZ) Method Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mono-Crystal Furnace market consists of two main types: the Czochralski (CZ) Method Furnace and the Floating Zone (FZ) Method Furnace. The CZ method involves pulling a single crystal ingot from a molten silicon melt, commonly used for silicon wafer production. The FZ method involves passing a focused laser beam through a polycrystalline rod to melt and recrystallize it into a single crystal, often used for high-purity materials. Both methods play a crucial role in the production of mono-crystalline materials for various industries such as electronics and solar panels.</p></p>
