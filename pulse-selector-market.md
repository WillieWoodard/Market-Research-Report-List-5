<p><p>Pulse selectors are devices used in electronics to control the timing of signals in a circuit. There are two main types of pulse selector markets - Divider and External Trigger. Divider pulse selectors divide an input signal into multiple output pulses, allowing for precise timing control. External Trigger pulse selectors rely on an external signal to trigger the generation of output pulses. Both types are essential in circuit design for applications requiring accurate timing and synchronization of signals.</p></p>
