<p><p>Carbon nanotubes (CNTs) are a revolutionary material with two main types based on their structures: Single-Walled Carbon Nanotubes (SWNTs) and Multi-Walled Carbon Nanotubes (MWNTs). SWNTs are composed of a single graphene sheet rolled into a cylindrical shape, while MWNTs consist of multiple nested graphene sheets. These two types have different properties and applications, with SWNTs being preferred for their unique electrical and mechanical properties, and MWNTs for their strength and resilience in various industries such as electronics, aerospace, and energy storage.</p></p>
