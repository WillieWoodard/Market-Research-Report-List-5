<p><p>Synthetic Single Crystal Diamond refers to man-made diamonds produced using two main methods - HPHT (High Pressure High Temperature) and CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition). HPHT diamonds are created by subjecting carbon to high pressure and temperature to mimic the natural process of diamond formation, resulting in high quality diamonds suitable for industrial and gemstone applications. CVD diamonds, on the other hand, are grown by vaporizing carbon-rich gases and depositing them onto a substrate, offering high purity and control over diamond characteristics for various industrial uses.</p></p>
