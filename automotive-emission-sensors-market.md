<p><strong>The Automotive Emission Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>O2-Lambda Sensors</li><li>NOX Sensors</li><li>Particulate Matter Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Emission Sensors play a crucial role in monitoring and controlling emissions from vehicles. The three main types of emission sensors are O2-Lambda Sensors, NOX Sensors, and Particulate Matter Sensors. O2-Lambda Sensors measure the oxygen content in the exhaust gas to help optimize fuel efficiency. NOX Sensors monitor and reduce the levels of nitrogen oxides emitted by the vehicle. Particulate Matter Sensors detect and regulate the particulate matter emissions, such as soot, ensuring cleaner exhaust gases. These sensors are essential for vehicles to comply with emission regulations and reduce environmental impact.</p></p>
