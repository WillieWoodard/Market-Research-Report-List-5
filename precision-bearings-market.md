<p><ul><li>Ball Bearing</li><li>Roller Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Precision bearings can be categorized into two main types: ball bearings and roller bearings. Ball bearings use balls to maintain separation between the inner and outer race, providing low friction and smooth rotation. Roller bearings, on the other hand, use cylindrical rollers instead of balls to facilitate higher load capacity and better shock resistance. Both types of bearings are crucial in industrial machinery and equipment, ensuring precision performance and longevity.</p></p>
