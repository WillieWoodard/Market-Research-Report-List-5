<p><ul><li>Invasive Leak Detection</li><li>Non-Invasive Leak Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Invasive leak detection equipment involves methods that require direct contact with the liquid in order to detect leaks, such as using sensors or probes placed within the liquid. On the other hand, non-invasive leak detection equipment does not require direct contact with the liquid and instead relies on technologies like infrared imaging or acoustic sensors to detect leaks. Both types of equipment aim to accurately and efficiently detect leaks in various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
