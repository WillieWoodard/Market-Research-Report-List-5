<p><p>Rotational Vestibular Chairs are specialized chairs designed for vestibular therapy. These chairs come in three main types - Manual Wholebody Rotation Chair, Passive Headon-body Rotation Chair, and Active Headon-body Rotation Chair. </p><p>The Manual Wholebody Rotation Chair requires the user to manually rotate their entire body. Passive Headon-body Rotation Chairs rotate the user's head and body together without any effort on their part. Active Headon-body Rotation Chairs require the user to actively rotate their head and body, providing a more engaging therapy experience.</p></p>
