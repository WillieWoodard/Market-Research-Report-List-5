<p><p>Hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) is a simulation technique used in the development and testing of complex control systems. By combining the physical and virtual components, engineers can accurately assess the performance of the system under various conditions before deployment.</p><p>The Hardware-in-the-loop Market is experiencing significant growth, with the market expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.7% during the forecast period.
