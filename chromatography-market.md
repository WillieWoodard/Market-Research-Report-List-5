<p><p>Chromatography market includes various types such as Liquid Chromatography (LC), Gas Chromatography (GC), Supercritical Fluid Chromatography (SFC), and Thin Layer Chromatography. Liquid Chromatography involves separation of compounds in a liquid mobile phase, while Gas Chromatography separates compounds in a gaseous mobile phase. Supercritical Fluid Chromatography utilizes supercritical fluids as the mobile phase, and Thin Layer Chromatography separates compounds on a thin layer of adsorbent material. These different types cater to various industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, environmental analysis, and more.</p></p>
