<p><p>Underwater location beacons are used to locate and recover items such as Cockpit Voice Recorders (CVRs), Flight Data Recorders (FDRs), and other valuable equipment that may be lost at sea. The CVR records conversations and other sounds in the cockpit, while the FDR records flight data parameters. Other types of beacons may include emergency locator transmitters or personal locator beacons. These devices are crucial in aiding search and rescue operations and ensuring the safety and security of marine operations.</p></p>
