<p><p>Eccentric disk valves are commonly categorized into two market types: wafer type and lug type. Wafer type valves are designed to fit between two flanges without the need for additional fasteners, making them easy to install and remove. Lug type valves have threaded inserts on either side of the valve body to enable easy installation and removal from piping systems. Both types offer excellent performance in controlling flow and are widely used in various industrial applications.</p></p>
