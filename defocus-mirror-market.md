<p><p>Defocus Mirror Market Types include Peripheral Defocus and Multipoint Defocus mirrors. Peripheral Defocus mirrors are designed to redirect light away from the central part of the mirror, creating a blurry peripheral image. This type of mirror is commonly used in surveillance systems and art installations. On the other hand, Multipoint Defocus mirrors have multiple focal points, creating distorted reflections and enhancing artistic or aesthetic appeal in interior design and decorative applications. Both types offer unique visual effects and creative possibilities in various industries.</p></p>
