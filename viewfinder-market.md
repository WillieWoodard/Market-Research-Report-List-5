<p><ul><li>Optical Viewfinder</li><li>Electronic Viewfinder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Viewfinder Market: Optical viewfinders use mirrors and prisms to reflect the scene through the lens to the photographer's eye, providing a natural and clear view of the subject. They are often found in DSLR cameras and are favored by many photographers for their accuracy and lack of lag.</p><p>Electronic Viewfinder Market: Electronic viewfinders use a small display screen to digitally recreate the scene through the lens. They offer additional features such as exposure simulation and focus peaking. They are commonly found in mirrorless cameras and are preferred by some photographers for their ability to show real-time adjustments to exposure settings.</p></p>
