<p><p>The Solid Surface Material market consists of two main types: Casting Molding Solid Surface and Extrusion Molding Solid Surface. Casting Molding Solid Surface involves pouring a liquid resin mixture into a mold and allowing it to harden into a solid surface product. Extrusion Molding Solid Surface is created by pushing heated resin through a die to produce a continuous shape. Both types of solid surface materials offer unique characteristics and advantages for various applications in construction, interior design, and other industries.</p></p>
