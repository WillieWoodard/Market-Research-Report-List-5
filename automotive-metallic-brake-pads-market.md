<p><p>Automotive metallic brake pads are divided into two main types: Semi-Metallic Brake Pads and Low Metallic NAO Brake Pads. </p><p>Semi-Metallic Brake Pads are made with a combination of metal fibers and other materials like graphite and friction modifiers. They offer strong stopping power and are durable, making them suitable for high-performance vehicles.</p><p>Low Metallic NAO (Non-Asbestos Organic) Brake Pads contain a lower percentage of metal fibers, which results in less noise and dust compared to semi-metallic pads. They provide good braking performance and are commonly used in everyday vehicles.</p></p>
