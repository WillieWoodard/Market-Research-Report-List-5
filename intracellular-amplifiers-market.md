<p><p>Intracellular amplifiers are used for recording and amplifying signals from individual cells. Single channel amplifiers are designed to amplify signals from one cell at a time, providing high resolution and sensitivity. Two channel amplifiers allow for simultaneous recording from two cells, enabling researchers to compare and analyze data in real-time. Both types of amplifiers are essential tools in electrophysiology research, allowing scientists to study the electrical activity of cells in detail and uncover important insights into cellular function and communication.</p></p>
