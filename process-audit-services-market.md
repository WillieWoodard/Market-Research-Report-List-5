<p><p>Process Audit Service market can be divided into two types: Series Process Audit Service and Parallel Process Audit Service. In Series Process Audit Service, auditing of processes occurs sequentially, one after the other. This type of audit service ensures a thorough examination of each process individually. On the other hand, Parallel Process Audit Service involves auditing multiple processes simultaneously. This allows for a quicker assessment of the overall efficiency and effectiveness of the processes being audited.</p></p>
