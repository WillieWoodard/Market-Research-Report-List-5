<p><p>Elastic adhesives and sealants market consists of three main types: polyurethane, silicone, and silyl modified polymers (SMP). Polyurethane adhesives and sealants offer excellent flexibility, durability, and chemical resistance. Silicone adhesives and sealants are known for their high heat resistance and excellent weathering properties. SMP adhesives and sealants combine the best attributes of polyurethane and silicone, offering high flexibility, adhesion, and resistance to UV and weathering. Each type caters to different applications and industries, providing a wide range of choices for consumers.</p></p>
