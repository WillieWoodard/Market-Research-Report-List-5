<p><ul><li>Class A Laminated Glass</li><li>Class B Laminated Glass</li><li>Tempered Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive side glass market consists of three main types: Class A laminated glass, Class B laminated glass, and tempered glass. Class A laminated glass is made up of two layers of glass with a layer of polyvinyl butyral (PVB) sandwiched between them, providing high levels of safety and sound insulation. Class B laminated glass also contains a layer of PVB but is thinner and lighter than Class A. Tempered glass is created through a process of rapid heating and cooling, making it stronger and safer than standard glass.</p></p>
