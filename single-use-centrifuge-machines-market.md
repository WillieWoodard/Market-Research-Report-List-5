<p><p>Single-use centrifuge machines are commonly categorized into two main types: benchtop centrifuges and continuous flow centrifuges. Benchtop centrifuges are smaller in size, designed for lower volume processing of samples in laboratory settings. On the other hand, continuous flow centrifuges are larger machines that are used for high-volume sample processing in industrial and research settings. Both types of centrifuges play a crucial role in separating particles based on their density through high-speed spinning.</p></p>
