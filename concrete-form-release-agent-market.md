<p><strong>The Concrete Form Release Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Mold Releases</li><li>External Mold Releases</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concrete Form Release Agent market can be categorized into two types: Internal Mold Releases and External Mold Releases. Internal Mold Releases are added directly to the concrete mix to prevent the bonding of the concrete to the formwork. External Mold Releases are applied to the surface of the formwork before pouring the concrete to ensure easy removal of the formwork once the concrete has set. Both types of form release agents help in achieving smooth and high-quality concrete finishes.</p></p>
