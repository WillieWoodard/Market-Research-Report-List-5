<p><p>Vacuum Metallizing Equipment market comprises two main types: Suspension Type and Roller Type. Suspension Type equipment involves suspending the object to be metallized within the vacuum chamber, allowing for uniform coating on all sides. Roller Type equipment, on the other hand, involves rolling the object through the vacuum chamber to ensure even coating. Both types of equipment are widely used in industries such as automotive, electronics, and packaging for applications like decorative coatings, reflective surfaces, and barrier films.</p></p>
