<p><p>Helicopter engines can be categorized into three main types: piston engines, gas turbine engines, and stamping engines. Piston engines are commonly used in smaller helicopters and are simpler and less expensive. Gas turbine engines are more powerful and efficient, making them suitable for larger helicopters and military applications. Stamping engines are a newer technology that utilizes electromagnetic forces to drive the engine, offering potential advantages in terms of efficiency and emissions. Each type of engine has its own unique characteristics and is suited for different applications in the helicopter industry.</p></p>
