<p><strong>The Industrial Communication Gateways Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Network Connecting</li><li>Device Connecting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial Communication Gateways come in two main types: Network Connecting and Device Connecting. Network Connecting gateways facilitate communication between different networks, such as Ethernet, Profibus, or Modbus. They enable seamless data transfer across various protocols, ensuring smooth operations in industrial settings. Device Connecting gateways, on the other hand, allow different devices, such as sensors, actuators, or controllers, to communicate with each other, even if they use different communication protocols. These gateways help integrate diverse devices into a cohesive system, enhancing overall efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
