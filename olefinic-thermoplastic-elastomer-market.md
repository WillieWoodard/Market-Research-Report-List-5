<p><p>Olefinic Thermoplastic Elastomers are a type of material known for their elasticity, durability, and ease of processing. Two common types of Olefinic Thermoplastic Elastomers are Thermoplastic Polyolefin (TPO) and Thermoplastic Vulcanizates. TPOs are a blend of polypropylene and EPDM rubber, offering weather resistance and impact strength. Thermoplastic Vulcanizates combine the properties of rubber with the processing advantages of thermoplastics, resulting in a material that is flexible, resistant to chemicals, and has good mechanical properties.</p></p>
