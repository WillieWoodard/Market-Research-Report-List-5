<p><p>Oil-air coolers are commonly categorized into oil-type and dry-type coolers. Oil-type coolers utilize oil as the cooling medium and are typically used in heavy-duty industrial applications where high heat dissipation is required. On the other hand, dry-type coolers use air as the cooling medium and are suitable for applications where water usage or contamination is a concern. Both types of coolers play a crucial role in maintaining optimal operating temperatures for various industrial equipment, ensuring efficient performance and prolonged lifespan.</p></p>
