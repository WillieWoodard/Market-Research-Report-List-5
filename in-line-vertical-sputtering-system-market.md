<p><ul><li>In-line Vertical Static Sputtering System</li><li>In-line Vertical Dynamic Sputtering System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-line Vertical Sputtering Systems are used for depositing thin films on substrates in a continuous production process. In-line Vertical Static Sputtering Systems are designed for batch processing, where substrates remain stationary during the sputtering process. In contrast, In-line Vertical Dynamic Sputtering Systems involve substrates moving continuously through the sputtering chamber, resulting in higher throughput and efficiency. Both types of systems offer unique advantages and are utilized in various industries for applications such as semiconductor manufacturing and optical coatings.</p></p>
