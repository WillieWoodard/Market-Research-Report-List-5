<p><p>1,4 Butanediol (BDO) is produced through various processes including the Reppe process, Davy process, Butadiene process, and Propylene oxide process. The Reppe process involves the reaction of acetylene and formaldehyde, while the Davy process uses maleic anhydride and ethanol. The Butadiene process utilizes butadiene and yields BDO as a byproduct. The Propylene oxide process involves the hydration of 1,3-butadiene. Each process has its own advantages and applications in the market for 1,4 Butanediol.</p></p>
