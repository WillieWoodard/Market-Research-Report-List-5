<p><ul><li>Mobile Bucket</li><li>Overhead System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concrete distribution system market includes two main types: mobile bucket systems and overhead systems. Mobile bucket systems involve a crane-mounted bucket that transports and discharges concrete over a wide area, providing flexibility and mobility on construction sites. On the other hand, overhead systems use a network of pipelines and conveyors to transport concrete from a central mixing plant to various locations, ensuring efficient and continuous distribution. These systems cater to different construction needs and requirements for concrete placement.</p></p>
