<p><p>The Clavicle Compression Plate market consists of three main types: Clavicle Shaft Plate, Clavicle Hook Plate, and Clavicle Superior Lateral Plate. The Clavicle Shaft Plate is specifically designed for fractures located in the middle of the clavicle, providing stability and support. The Clavicle Hook Plate is used for complex fractures near the shoulder, offering additional fixation. The Clavicle Superior Lateral Plate is used for fractures near the acromioclavicular joint, providing superior lateral support and stability.</p></p>
