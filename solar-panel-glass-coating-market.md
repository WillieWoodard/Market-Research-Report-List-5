Hydrophilic coatings attract water molecules, allowing for more efficient cleaning and maintenance. On the other hand, hydrophobic coatings repel water, reducing dirt and dust buildup on the panels. Both types of coatings offer benefits in terms of improving energy efficiency and durability of solar panels in different weather conditions.</p></p>
