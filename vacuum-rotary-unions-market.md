<p><p>Vacuum rotary unions are devices that allow for the transfer of gases or liquids between two rotating parts. There are two main types of vacuum rotary unions: Single Channel Vacuum Rotary Unions and Dual Channel Vacuum Rotary Unions. Single channel unions have one passage for the transfer of gas or liquid, while dual channel unions have two separate passages. Single channel unions are often used in simpler applications where only one fluid needs to be transferred, while dual channel unions are used when two separate fluids need to be transferred simultaneously.</p></p>
