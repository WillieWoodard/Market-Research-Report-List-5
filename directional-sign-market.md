<p><p>Directional signs come in various market types such as wall-mounted and floor-standing. Wall-mounted directional signs are typically fixed to walls or ceilings to provide guidance to customers in indoor environments. They are often used in hallways, corridors, and entrances. On the other hand, floor-standing directional signs are freestanding structures placed on the floor to direct foot traffic in larger spaces like lobbies, shopping centers, or event venues. Both types are essential for helping people navigate complex or unfamiliar environments efficiently.</p></p>
