<p><p>Application Delivery Controllers (ADCs) are networking devices that work as a part of application delivery network to manage and optimize application traffic. With the growing trend of digital transformation and the increasing importance of application availability and security, the demand for ADCs is expected to continue to rise.</p><p>Key trends in the Application Delivery Controllers Market include the increasing adoption of Software-defined Networking (SDN) and Network Function Virtualization (NFV) technologies, the rise of application-centric networking, and the integration of machine learning and AI capabilities in ADCs for improved performance and security.
