<p><p>Continuous tempering furnaces are used in heat treatment processes for materials like glass, metal, and ceramics. The market is segmented into three main types: pusher type furnace, conveyor type furnace, and other types. Pusher type furnaces have a stationary heating chamber and pusher mechanism to move materials through. Conveyor type furnaces have a continuous belt system to transport materials through the heating chamber. Other types may include rotary hearth furnaces or mesh belt furnaces. Each type offers unique benefits for different manufacturing processes.</p></p>
