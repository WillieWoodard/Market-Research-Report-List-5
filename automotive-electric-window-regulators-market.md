<p><p>The automotive electric window regulators market can be categorized into different types based on the mechanism used for operation. The common types include scissor regulators, cable regulators, flexible shaft regulators, and others. Scissor regulators utilize a scissor-like mechanism to raise and lower the window. Cable regulators use cables to control the movement of the window. Flexible shaft regulators use a flexible shaft to operate the window. Other types may include variations or combinations of these mechanisms to regulate the movement of automotive windows.</p></p>
