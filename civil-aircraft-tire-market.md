<p><p>The civil aircraft tire market can be segmented into two main types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires have cords that run at a 90-degree angle to the direction of travel, providing greater flexibility and heat dissipation, which results in improved performance and fuel efficiency. Bias tires have cords that run diagonally across the tire, offering a stiffer construction and higher load-carrying capacity. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different aircraft applications based on requirements for durability, performance, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
