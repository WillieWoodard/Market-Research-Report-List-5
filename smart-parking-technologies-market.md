<p><p>Smart parking technologies market can be classified into three main types: Ultrasonic sensor, radar sensor, and image sensor. Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to detect the presence of vehicles in parking spaces. Radar sensors use radio waves to determine the availability of parking spots. Image sensors capture images of parking spaces to analyze occupancy. Each of these technologies offer unique advantages and are used in various smart parking systems to improve efficiency and convenience for both drivers and parking facility operators.</p></p>
