<p><p>Rotary actuators are used to convert hydraulic power into rotational motion. Single-cylinder hydraulic rotary actuators have a simple design with one cylinder responsible for producing rotary motion. Multi-cylinder hydraulic rotary actuators have multiple cylinders working together to generate rotational force, providing more power and control. Other types of rotary actuators include rack-and-pinion actuators, vane actuators, and piston actuators, each offering unique features and capabilities for various applications. These different types cater to a wide range of industries and requirements for rotational movement.</p></p>
