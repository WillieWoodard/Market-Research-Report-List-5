<p><p>Digital printing wallpaper market consists of two main types: Inkjet and Electrophotography. Inkjet printing involves using small droplets of ink to create images on wallpaper, offering high quality and vibrant colors. Electrophotography, on the other hand, uses electrostatic charges to transfer toner onto paper, providing crisp and precise images. Both technologies are popular in the wallpaper industry for their ability to produce customized designs quickly and efficiently. Customers can choose between Inkjet and Electrophotography options based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
