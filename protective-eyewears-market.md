<p><p>Protective eyewear market can be divided into three main types: direct vented, indirect vented, and non-vented. Direct vented protective eyewear have vents that allow air flow while protecting against particles and liquids. Indirect vented protective eyewear have vents that prevent liquids and particles from entering the eye area. Non-vented protective eyewear do not have vents and provide the highest level of protection against airborne hazards. Each type caters to different needs and levels of protection for various industries and activities.</p></p>
